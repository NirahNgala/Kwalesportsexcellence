Twalenga Women in Sports Campaign

Empowering women and girls through sports by breaking barriers and promoting gender equality. We create opportunities for female athletes through mentorship, training, and advocacy.

Sports Safety Campaign

Ensuring a safe environment for all athletes by promoting injury prevention, safety gear, and proper training practices. We work with coaches, medical professionals, and the community.
