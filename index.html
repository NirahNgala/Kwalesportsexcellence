Our Programs

Sports Safety

The launch of a sports safety campaign in Kwale County is a response to the rise in preventable sports injuries, including fatalities, linked to the increasing popularity of local sports tournaments.

The campaign aims to address critical safety issues, including:

  • Lack of adequate safety gear
  • Hazardous playing environments
  • Disregard for established safety rules
  • Absence of trained first responders

Key initiatives will focus on:

  • Thorough identification of injury causes
  • Implementation of comprehensive safety programs
  • Education and training for players and coaches
  • Provision of essential safety resources

Our core principle: Player safety is paramount.

Sports for gender equality

We believe sports are a powerful platform for social change. Our Sports for Gender Equality program champions the inclusion and empowerment of women and girls in sports at all levels. We address the systemic barriers that limit their participation, fostering a culture of respect and equal opportunity.

At the heart of this initiative is the Twalenga Women in Sports Campaign. "Twalenga," meaning "Let's Aim High," embodies our commitment to breaking down stereotypes and providing women with the resources and support they need to excel. This campaign focuses on creating safe spaces, providing specialized training, and celebrating the achievements of female athletes, inspiring a new generation to pursue their sporting dreams.
