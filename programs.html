Save Our Sables Campaign

Sports have proven to have the ability to champion social causes around the world. Time and time again, sports have been used to rally communities, raise awareness, and fundraise by providing platforms for important issues.

The Save Our Sables campaign is a coordinated initiative to conserve the rare Roosevelt Sable Antelope (Hippotragus Niger Roosevelt), found only in the Shimba Hills National Reserve, Kwale County.

“The Sable is not just an animal. It is a living memory, a breath of the wild soul of Kwale. If it vanishes, a piece of us goes with it.”
