Sports and Mental Health

While sports should promote mental wellness, athletes often face performance pressure leading to issues like anxiety and burnout. Inspired by Rowan McKellar's perspective, we're addressing these challenges by using sports as a tool for mental health awareness, advocating for techniques like meditation, team bonding and professional support.
